Sample natural-language tasks to exercise router selection toward the healthcare workflow
(meta.id: healthcare_commercial_brief). Run from agentic-orchestration-tool/ with Ollama up, e.g.:
  python main.py "…prompt below…"

1) Compare connected infusion pumps vs smart IV workflow platforms for a 400-bed hospital: integration burden, cybersecurity, and what we should pilot first.

2) Our health system is evaluating remote patient monitoring for CHF: what vendor-agnostic architecture patterns reduce clinician alarm fatigue and what should be in the RFP?

3) Draft a connected-care strategy outline for oncology supportive care at home: devices, data flows, and how we avoid duplicating the EHR.

4) We are standardizing cellular-enabled devices across sites: what decision matrix should IT and procurement use when Wi‑Fi is unreliable in older buildings?

5) Commercial brief request: how do hospitals typically budget for SaaS per bed vs per device for RPM, and what questions should finance ask a vendor (no dollar amounts—framework only)?

6) Interoperability angle: for a new RPM rollout, what are the realistic US paths (FHIR APIs, vendor gateways, manual exports) and the main risks of each?
